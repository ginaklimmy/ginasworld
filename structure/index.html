perfect binding
Binder clip all pages, leave spine open. Score whole spine with xacto. Book Binding glue (put in bowl). with paintbrush, paint thin coat of glue accross the spine perpendicular to pages. pay attention to top and bottom. Binder clip spine edge pressure on edge. let sit for 20-30 min. score again lightly. another light layer of glue with brush. binder clip again on edge. 3-4 glue layers on spine. cover: measure spine and prescore spine on cover page. binder clip.
Saddle stitching
Awl tool, cradle tool , embroidery needles (thicker than regular), wax thread. Fold all pages first along spine, make them edges crispy. Place folded pages in the cradle with pole on top

Lemon Loaf:


[x] FLOUR 1.5 cups
[x] BAKING POWDER .5 teaspoon
[x] BAKING SODA .25 teaspoon
[x] SALT .25 teaspoon
[ ] UNSALTED BUTTER (softened) .5 cup
[x] SUGAR granulated 1 cup
[x] EGGS 3
[x] VANILLA EXTRACT .5 teaspoon
[ ] LEMON EXTRACT 1 teaspoon
[ ] 1 Large Lemon (the zest)
[ ] LEMON JUICE 2 tablespoons
[ ] BUTTERMILK 1/3 cup (sub sour cream)
Lemon Icing
[x] POWDERED SUGAR 1+ cups
[ ] LEMON JUICE 1 tablespoons
[ ] CREAM/ MILK 1 tablespoon
Lemon Loaf
1. whisk flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt. 2. whisk together butter and sugar for three minutes
3. mix eggs in one at a time
4. mix in the vanilla extract, lemon extract, lemon zest, and lemon juice
5. mix in .5 of the flour mixture and .5 of the butter milk
6. repeat
7. pour in pan and bake 1 hr
Lemon Icing
1. whisk together powdered sugar, lemon juice, and cream/milk, add more as needed 2
. drizzle over lemon loaf
